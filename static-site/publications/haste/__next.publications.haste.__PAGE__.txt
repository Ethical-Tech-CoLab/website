3b:["$","p","25",{"children":[["$","span",null,{"className":"font-semibold text-accent","children":"Damage threshold, default 0.1."}]," ","The proportion of damaged pixels above which a building is declared damaged. 3f:["$","p","29",{"children":[["$","span",null,{"className":"font-semibold text-accent","children":"The final estimate."}]," ","HASTE does not simply count the buildings its model called damaged and publish that number.
